How to Play Quoridor

  • Goal: Reach the opposite side of the board before your opponent.
  • Move: Click a space to move your pawn one step (orthogonally).
  • Place Wall: Click Place Wall, then select two adjacent wall slots and confirm. Walls block paths but cannot fully block a player.
  • Turn: Players alternate turns. You can move or place a wall on your turn.
  • Win: First to reach the opposite edge wins the round. Scoreboard tracks wins.
  • Tip: Use walls strategically to slow your opponent, but don't block yourself!
